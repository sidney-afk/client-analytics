## ⚠️ FLAGGED — real issues I did NOT change (need your decision / a bigger change)

- 🐞 **MED — Undated posts pass *every* specific-month filter** on the Sheet, so an
  undated card shows under "June". *Fix (one line):* `return false` for no-date when
  a concrete month is selected. Behavior change — your call.
- 🐞 **MED — Reorder slot-healing can collide with a hidden post's `order_index`**,
  causing a nondeterministic snap-back on reload. *Fix:* heal against the union of
  all order_index (incl. hidden), or use spaced/fractional slots.
- ⚡ **MED — Every realtime row change = full client re-fetch + full calendar
  re-render.** Softened by an equality gate + thumb-harvest, but a real teammate
  change rebuilds the whole strip. *Fix (architectural):* a per-row patch path.
- ⚡ **LOW — Caption poller doesn't pause when the tab is hidden**; a stalled job
  polls 5 s + a 1 s ticker for up to 12 min. *Fix:* gate the poll on `document.hidden`.
- 🐞 **LOW — Removing YouTube from a card mid-title-review strands** `title_status`
  (drops from queues but data persists, unreachable). *Fix:* confirm/clear on removal.
- 💡 **LOW — Platforms editor lets you save an EMPTY set** → strip + per-card toggles
  vanish everywhere (recoverable via the kebab). *Fix:* require ≥1 or confirm.
- 🐞 **LOW — Restored caption job force-failed** after >3 min closed even if the
  backend finished; restore also re-shows the "Generating X/Y" chip for old jobs.
- 💡 **LOW — Cancel is best-effort** (a caption can still land after the UI says
  "cancelled"); a **manual edit during generation silently drops** the generated one
  with no "use generated?" affordance.
- 🐞 **LOW — Kasper reply send has no in-flight guard** (double-submit only
  prevented incidentally); toggling show-all / mark-read re-renders the whole inbox.
- ⚠️ Note: the Kasper queue realtime channel subscribes to ALL clients' rows
  (intentional for the cross-client tab, but the global fan-out to keep an eye on).

## ✅ Checked and found SOLID (not bugs)
- Client/read-only guards for select-mode, archive/link/delete buttons, and kebab
  editors — clients can't reach SMM mutations.
- Month/Week boundary math is timezone-consistent; archived cards filtered at load
  for all views; filters + scroll + selection persist/restore correctly.
- Caption bulk gen is not all-or-nothing; concurrency capped at 2; jobs cleaned up;
  save-slug pinned (no cross-client caption leak).
- **Kasper cross-client safety is clean** — replies route by `item.slug`, are
  internal/Kasper-role, hidden from clients by `_calCommentsForView`, and every
  handler resolves a pid within its own loaded per-client list (stale pid no-ops).
- Whole app renders with **0 JS errors** across SMM (4 views), client, and Kasper.
